Who We Are

Sweet sound,
serious soul.

Nutella is a cool, classy live band built around the voice of Kia — a singer who can hush a room or set it on fire. Backed by two guitarists and a groove that refuses to sit still, we bring warmth to every stage we step on.

From candle-lit pubs to packed events, our whole reason for being is simple: spread the love of music to as many people as possible — and remind everyone that a great song is something we all share.

The Nutella Promise

Pubs, private parties, corporate nights, weddings, open-air events — if you've got a crowd, we've got the set. We're ready to perform wherever you want us.

Why We Do This

More than a show — a kind of healing.

Our mission goes beyond a good night out. We're building Nutella into a force for music therapy — using sound to help people climb out of sadness and depression, and walk away lighter, brighter and more cheerful.

🎶

Spread the Music

We bring live music to people who might never set foot in a concert hall — pubs, streets, community events. Music belongs to everyone.

💛

Music as Therapy

A formal step we're working toward: sessions and shows designed to ease anxiety, lift low moods, and help people feel something good again.

✨

Leave People Cheerful

Every set has one goal — that you leave happier than you came. We want to help people become better, lighter versions of themselves.
